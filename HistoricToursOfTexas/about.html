Our Mission & Values

What drives us to share Texas's incredible history

Learn Local History

Discover authentic Texas and Mexican history through visits to historic museums, sites, and landmarks. Our expert guides research extensively to bring factual, compelling stories to life.

Small Groups

Join intimate groups of 8 to 12 people for a personalized experience. Our small group size ensures meaningful interactions and the ability to explore special sites away from large crowds.

The Best Cuisine

Enjoy the best local cuisine during your tours. From authentic regional dishes to farm-to-table dining, we include culinary experiences that tell their own stories about Texas heritage.

Sanitized & Safe

For your safety, the Joye Mobile is fully sanitized and disinfected before each tour. We prioritize your health and comfort as we travel through Texas's most historic destinations.

Cozy Lodging

Stay at carefully selected cozy bed & breakfasts or luxury hotels. We handpick accommodations that complement your tour experience and add to the authentic charm of each destination.

The Joye Mobile

Travel in comfort and style with the Joye Mobile - our 12-passenger limo-seating van. Specially equipped for comfort, it's designed to be the perfect vehicle for your Texas history adventure.
